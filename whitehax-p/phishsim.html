WhiteHaX PhishSim Platform

WhiteHaX PhishSim is complete cloud-based platform for training, simulation and verification of business employees on the risks of Phishing and how to detect and block Phishing scams. Training is performed using interactive tutorials, games and quizzes. Verification is done through simulating hundreds of actual, real-life Phishing Scams from Microsoft Office365 to Banking, HR, corporate and other scams to verify employee readiness against phishing. Everything from whether the employee completed training, played games, took quizzes to whether they opened phishing emails, clicked any embedded URL links or attachments and entered any prsonal data, is all tracked. Comprehensive reports then allow CIO/CSO to verify the business readiness against Phishing & where the business might be vulnerable by groups or individual users.


Phishing & BEC Threats:

The most prolific and commonly used cyber attack techniques are Phishing, Spear-phishing and Business Email Compromise (BEC) scams. In 2020, BEC & Phishing scams cost businesses over $2B in cyber crime losses – that’s 45% of all cyber crimes losses in 2020. In fact, Email based breaches due to human-error such as business compromise, ransomware/malware spread, identity theft etc. are the largest category of all cyber breaches, compromises or attacks which have resulted in most monies paid out by the cyber insurance companies. Training employees on how to recognize email based threats, helps substantially in eliminating the risk of compromise. Based on several business surveys, employee training and verification results in over 70% risk reduction from email based business threats such as phishing & BEC.


WhiteHaX – Phishing & BEC readiness verification platform:

: In order to help businesses train & verify their employees on Phishing & BEC risks, IronSDN has developed the low-cost, purpose-built, cloud-based, multi-hosting WhiteHaX Phish-Sim platform – built on simple principal of Educate, Demonstrate, Verify. WhiteHaX Phish-Sim allows businesses to a) train users through interactive tutorials and phishing games; b) simulate phishing & BEC scams through actual emails sent to employees; c) verify which group of employees are the most susceptible and d) measure overall business readiness against Phishing & BECs. Following are the four major components of the WhiteHaX Phish-Sim platform;

1

Interactive, Easy-to-grasp Trainings:

Deep Analysis of Security & Data Privacy of Mobile Device through periodic verification of over 50 different OS and application security settings, data privacy, network security and user threats in less than 2 minutes.

2

Employee Certification:

Once training is complete, employees are prompted to take quizzes and play various games verify employee’s understanding of the phishing and BEC scams. With every completed quiz or game employees gain multi-level certification, which in turn is tracked.

3

Real-life Email Simulations

WhiteHaX Platform includes dozens of email templates which simulate real life phishing & BEC scams. Templates can simulate email fraud in many categories such as Microsoft O365 & SharePoint scams, various user lure techniques, business compromise scams such as invoice fraud, payroll redirect, executive requests etc.; social networking scams and others. IronSDN is constantly adding new templates to simulate most recent & most dangerous email scams so that business can keep employees trained to detect such scams

4

Extensive Verification & Reporting:

WhiteHaX platform tracks all user activities including whether training & various quiz/games were completed, every simulated email that’s sent to an employee, whether user clicked the attachment or URL in the email, whether any data was input in to a fake website and what kind of data etc. Business IT team then can roll up the reports to evaluate phishing & BEC readiness for overall business, groups or individual employees. This allows the business not only to understand which groups of users are more vulnerable than others, but also get employees well prepared to detect and prevent any email based threats.
